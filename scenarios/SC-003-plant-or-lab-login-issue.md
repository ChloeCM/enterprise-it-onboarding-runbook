# Scenario SC-003 - Plant or Lab User Cannot Log In

## Situation

New Starter assigned to a plant or lab location reports they cannot log into a shared workstation or required system.

## First-line Checks

- Confirm the users identity, role and assigned location.
- Comfirm correct workstation and login method is being used
- Check whether other users at the site are affected
- Confirm onboarding details were completed for the correct location/ site.

# Outcome

Issue identified as location specific access or configuration requirements. Escalated with site deatils and observed behaviour and informed user.
